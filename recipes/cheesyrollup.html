Cheesy Rollup Recipe

if you can roll up joint you can roll up a cheesy roll up

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Shredded cheese blend

Steps

  1. Place tortilla on a heated pan
  2. Delicately sprinkle the cheese on the tortilla
  3. Let the cheese melt on the tortilla
  4. Once the cheese is melted, remove from pan
  5. As you would a joint, roll up the tortilla
