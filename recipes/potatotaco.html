Spicy Potato Tacos Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Chipotle sauce
  • Sour cream
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Cubed potatoes

Steps

  1. Season the potatoes with whatever seasonings
  2. Air fry the potatoes until they're crispy
  3. Make or open a jar of chipotle sauce
  4. Toss the potatoes with the chipotle sauce
  5. Put the potatoes and toppings into a flour tortilla
