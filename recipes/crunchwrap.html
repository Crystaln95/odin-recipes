Crunchwrap SUPREME Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Seasoned beef
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Nacho cheese sauce
  • Crispy tostada shell
  • Flour tortilla

Steps

  1. Saute the beef with whatever seasonings you want as long as you actually season it
  2. Either make some cheese sauce or open a nice can of it
  3. Lay out the tortilla
  4. Layer the meat, cheese, tostada, and remaining ingredients
  5. Fold the tortilla around the ingredients
  6. Throw it on a pan and heat it up
  7. ENJOY!
